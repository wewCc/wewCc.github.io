英语段落赏析

翻译题目

扇子自古以来就深受中国人喜爱，但现在已不只是消暑纳凉的工具，而更多地作为艺术品供人欣赏。许多扇子造型优美、做工精良，并绘有山水、花鸟、人物等精美图案，具有很高的艺术价值。中国许多著名画家和书法家喜欢在扇子上作诗绘画，展示其艺术品味。扇子常作为礼物赠予他人，表达美好的祝福和真挚的情感。如今，扇子的实用功能已大为减弱，但作为一种文化符号和艺术形式，扇子仍然在中国传统文化中扮演着重要角色。

解析一

Fans have been cherished by the Chinese people since ancient times, but they are no longer merely tools for cooling off and enjoying the breeze; they have increasingly become objects of art for people to admire. Many fans are beautifully shaped, meticulously crafted, and adorned with exquisite patterns of landscapes, flowers and birds, and figures, which hold significant artistic value. Renowned Chinese painters and calligraphers often inscribe poems and paintings on fans to showcase their artistic tastes. Fans are frequently given as gifts to express good wishes and sincere emotions. Nowadays, although the practical function of fans has greatly diminished, as a cultural symbol and form of art, fans continue to play an important role in traditional Chinese culture.

知识积累(罗列出来的单词可以多用在写作中使用)

cherish 珍视,喜爱

no longer 不再是，不再

merely 仅仅

meticulously 细致地

craft 工艺,手艺,精心制作(动词)

exquisite 精致的，精美的

adorn 装饰(动词)

renowned 有名望的，著名的

inscribe 题写；题献；铭记

showcase展示（本领、优点等）的场合（名词） 展示，展现（动词）

解析二

Fans have been deeply loved by Chinese people since ancient times. However, now fans are not just a tool to beat the summer heat but are more often a work of art for people to appreciate. Many fans have elegant shapes and excellent craftsmanship and are painted with landscapes, flowers, birds, figures and other delicate patterns, possessing high artistic value. Many famous Chinese painters and calligraphers like to write poems and paint on fans to display their artistic taste. Fans are often given to others as gifts to express good wishes and sincere feelings. Nowadays, the practical function of fans has been greatly weakened, but as a cultural symbol and an art form, fans still play an important role in traditional Chinese culture.

知识积累

beat the summer heat 消暑 可以理解为抵御夏天的热量,所以为消暑。

since ancient times 自古以来 可以理解为从古时候开始

landscape 山水 这里原意指的是风景,景色,也可以作为山水的意思。

figure 人物,人像

这里注意,可以作为重要人物或者绘画模型中的人像的意思。平时指的大多是数字的意思。

pattern 图案,平日多作为模式的意思。

delicate 柔和的,小巧玲珑的,精巧的

taste 品味

practical function 实用功能

symbol 符号

weaken 使减弱,使削弱,使虚弱(动词)
